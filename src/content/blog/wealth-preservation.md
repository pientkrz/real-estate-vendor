## Building Generational Wealth

Luxury real estate serves as both lifestyle and asset. Properties in culturally significant locations with strong fundamentals outpace inflation and provide portfolio diversification.

### Strategic Approach

- **Location Premium**: Properties in culturally enduring destinations
- **Quality Preservation**: Maintenance that protects asset value
- **Tax Efficiency**: Structuring ownership for optimal outcomes

Real estate remains the preferred wealth vehicle for discerning families across generations.
