## Looking Ahead

Global economic stability and rising wealth concentration among ultra-high-net-worth individuals continue to support the luxury property market. Key markets show resilience and selective growth opportunities.

### Market Indicators

- **Global UHNWI Growth**: +3.5% year-over-year
- **Prime Location Demand**: Strong across Mediterranean and Alpine regions
- **Interest Rates**: Stable financing conditions support acquisition

Selective investment in prime properties remains one of the most reliable wealth preservation strategies available to sophisticated investors.
