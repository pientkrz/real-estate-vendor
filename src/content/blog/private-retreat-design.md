## The Private Sanctuary

A truly luxurious property transcends real estate—it becomes a personal refuge. Every element, from spatial flow to material selection, should serve the owner's vision of perfection.

### Design Essentials

- **Spatial Hierarchy**: Public spaces flow to private sanctuaries
- **Sensory Experience**: Careful attention to light, sound, and texture
- **Personal Expression**: Bespoke design reflecting owner's identity

The greatest luxury homes are as much about the owner as they are about the architecture.
