## Seaside Excellence

Coastal properties demand a unique approach. Salt air corrosion, dynamic weather patterns, and the premium on waterfront access create distinct investment challenges and opportunities.

### Critical Considerations

- **Exposure**: South-facing terraces capture Aegean light
- **Materials**: Marine-grade finishes for longevity
- **Privacy**: Strategic landscaping without blocking views

The best coastal estates embrace their environment while protecting against its elements.
