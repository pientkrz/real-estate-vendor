## The Shifting Landscape

The luxury real estate market in 2026 continues to show strong fundamentals, with Mediterranean and Alpine properties leading growth. Buyers are increasingly focused on properties with premium locations and exclusive access.

### Key Metrics

- **Price Appreciation**: 8-12% annually in prime European markets
- **Cap Rates**: 3-4% for luxury residential portfolios
- **Buyer Profile**: 65% international high-net-worth individuals

Investment in cornerstone properties near cultural hubs and natural beauty spots remains the safest long-term bet.
